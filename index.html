About Me

I'm Coach Lesedi

I offer young stars(boys & girls) post and preseason training, We specialize in football specific strength & conditioning training. We also do technical football training which often is coupled with agility/flexibility training. Last but not least we offer mental preparation together with gross motor and coordination programs.

We host different tournaments for different seasons, part of our strategy revolves around grouping kids from disadvantaged areas with kids in affluent areas and that's true for boys and girls. Our training sessions are offered per grouping or individually depending on the players needs and efforts.
